# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company to identify the key factors influencing customer attrition and provide actionable insights for customer retention.

## Business Problem

Customer churn significantly impacts revenue and customer acquisition costs. The objective of this project is to:

- Identify churn patterns
- Understand customer demographics
- Analyze service usage behavior
- Discover factors contributing to customer attrition
- Support retention strategies

## Key Findings

### Finding 1
Customers with month-to-month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn rates compared to long-term contract customers.

### Finding 2
Customers using electronic check payments show higher churn tendencies than customers using automatic payment methods.

### Finding 3
New customers with lower tenure are more likely to churn than long-term customers.

### Finding 4
Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn probability.

### Finding 5
Fiber optic internet users experience higher churn rates than DSL users.

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to annual or multi-year contracts.
- Offer loyalty incentives to low-tenure customers.
- Promote automatic payment methods.
- Monitor high-bill customers for proactive retention campaigns.
- Create targeted retention programs for fiber optic subscribers.
